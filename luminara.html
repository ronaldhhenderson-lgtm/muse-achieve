Application Ritual · Rituel d'Application · Rituale

The Art of
Application

01
Cleanse & Prepare

Begin on thoroughly cleansed, slightly damp skin. The elixir's water-soluble actives penetrate optimally when the skin barrier is open and hydrated. Pat dry — do not rub.

02
Warm & Activate

Dispense 2–3 drops onto fingertips. Gently warm between palms for 5 seconds. This activates the botanical actives and prepares them for deeper penetration.

03
Press & Diffuse

Press — never rub — the elixir across face, neck, and décolletage using upward strokes. Hold each area for 3 seconds to encourage absorption into the deeper dermis.

04
Allow to Set

Allow 90 seconds before applying any additional products. This is the critical window during which the peptide matrix anchors to the skin and the hyaluronic complex begins its 72-hour hydration cycle.
