Application Ritual · Rituel d'Application · Rituale di Applicazione

The Art of
Application

01
Prepare the Canvas
Cleanse and tone. Pat gently dry. Apply immediately after toning while skin retains slight moisture — the Luminara formula is architectured to bond with a hydrated dermal surface for maximum penetration.
02
Dispense with Intention
Place 2–3 drops into the palm. Allow to warm for 10 seconds. This activates the colloidal gold particles and begins releasing the aromatic plant essence complex — your olfactory signal that the ritual has begun.
03
Press — Never Pull
Press the elixir into skin using upward, outward motions. The ceramide and lecithin complex requires gentle warmth to integrate into the lipid barrier. Extend an additional drop to décolleté and neck.
04
Allow the Silence
Wait 90 seconds before proceeding. In this window, sodium hyaluronate draws atmospheric moisture into the dermis, the peptide complex activates collagen signalling pathways, and arbutin begins its luminosity work.
